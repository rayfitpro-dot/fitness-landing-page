What It Is

This Is Not Just a Fitness Program.

Iron Dad Method is a standard. A practical coaching system built for fathers who want to lose body fat, build strength, eat with structure, stay accountable, and become more consistent in the areas that matter most.

This is fitness coaching for dads who are done starting over.

Four Pillars

Train With Purpose

A clear strength-focused training structure built to help you lose body fat, keep muscle, and rebuild confidence in the gym.

Eat With Structure

Simple nutrition targets, high-protein meals, and realistic nutrition coaching for busy dads around family, work, and real life.

Stay Accountable

Weekly check-ins, progress tracking, and honest accountability coaching for fathers so you are not left guessing or drifting.

Lead From the Front

Become the kind of man your family can feel: stronger, calmer, more consistent, and more present in fatherhood, discipline, fitness, and leadership.

Built in Nashville

Fitness Coaching for Dads in Nashville and Online.

Iron Dad Method is based in the Nashville, Tennessee area and built for busy fathers who want structure, accountability, and a clear path back to strength.

Whether you are training at a local gym, walking your neighborhood, or rebuilding your routine from home, the standard is the same: show up, do the work, and lead from the front.

A Note From Ray

I Built This For Dads Who Know They Have More In Them.

I built Iron Dad Method because I know what it feels like to want more for yourself while carrying real responsibility at home.

Work, marriage, kids, bills, stress, fatigue — it is easy for dads to put themselves last and call it sacrifice. But your family does not need a burned-out version of you. They need the strongest, clearest, most disciplined version of you.

This is not about perfection. It is about rebuilding your standard one day at a time. If that resonates with you, you are in the right place.
